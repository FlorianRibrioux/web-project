Excellence in Digital Engineering

Located in Villejuif, Efrei is a leading engineering school specializing in computer science, digital technologies, and innovation. Through hands-on projects, international opportunities, and strong partnerships with major technology companies, students develop both technical expertise and real-world experience. Our programs encourage creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving to prepare graduates for the challenges of tomorrow’s digital world.

A Project-Based Learning Approach

At Efrei, learning goes beyond traditional lectures. Students work on collaborative projects, real-world case studies, and innovative challenges throughout their studies. This practical approach allows them to apply theoretical concepts directly while developing communication, leadership, and teamwork skills highly valued in the professional world. From software development projects to AI and cybersecurity challenges, students gain the experience needed to succeed in the tech industry.
